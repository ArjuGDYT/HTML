Building a PC gives you control over the type of work you want to do while allowing you to build something to your creativitiy.

Even though it may look expensive, you can control the budget you want to use to build it! It can range from as little as $500 to a much as $10,000.

That's why I have created this website! There are many useful tools such as videos, guides, and websites which give a full breakdown of what you need to build the PC of your dreams.

The parts needed for a PC:

  • PC Chasis/Case
  • Control Processing Unit (CPU)
  • Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)
  • Motherboard
  • Random Access Memory (RAM) /Memory
  • Storage (Hard Drive/Solid State Drive/M.2/etc)
  • Power Supply Unit (PSU)
  • System Cooling (Fans or Water Cooling Unit)
